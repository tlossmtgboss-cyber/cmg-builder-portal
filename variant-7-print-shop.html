Construction Loan

Borrower has applied to Lender for a construction loan ("Loan"). Borrower intends to pay the Contract price, using proceeds of the Loan from Lender. Lender will make the Loan in accordance with the terms of the Construction Contract.

Loan Administration

Construction Budget and Advancements

The Work shall proceed in accordance with: the blueprints, shop drawings, plans and specifications for the Work; the written itemization of the Work and the Contract Price; and the itemized description of each segment of the Work that sets the corresponding payment advancements as specified in the Construction Budget accepted by the Lender.

Change Orders

Any change in the Contract Price, change in the Work or change in the contract time must be set forth in a Change Order signed by Borrower and Builder. Builder will provide to Lender copies of all change orders, including cost overruns, for approval. Builder agrees to not complete any work not included in the Construction Contract and Approved Construction Budget until a written change order has been signed by Builder and the Borrower and Lender has approved it in writing.

Insurance During the Construction Period

Builder shall keep in place an active policy for Builder's General Liability Insurance (minimum $1M) and Workers' Compensation Insurance. Builder's Risk Insurance must be maintained throughout the course of construction.

Construction Loan Disbursement Request

The Lender does not advance funds for deposits on materials, up front purchase of materials, or stored materials. These are the Builder's responsibility. The Lender advances construction draws for work completed.

Maximum 6 post-closing inspections/draws + 1 final is CMG's policy and industry standard. Final disbursement no less than 10% of contract amount. Each draw request must be accompanied by a lien waiver or lien release.

Limits on Construction Loan Draw Requests

  • Initial deposit / soft cost draw at closing: max 10% of contract price ($84,750 in this case).
  • Lender shall have final approval on amount funded/approved for each Draw Request.
  • After each Draw, the Loan Commitment Amount remaining will be sufficient to complete all Improvements.
  • Final Disbursement will not be less than 10% of the Contract amount.

Right of Lender to Inspect

Lender shall have the right to inspect the Property and the Improvements at any time.
