Step 12 · Of 13

Builder Acknowledgment

By signing, you acknowledge the construction loan terms — fixed price contract, 6 draws + 1 final, advances on work-in-place only, and the rest of the agreement.

Construction Loan & Contract. Builder agrees the Contract is fixed-price and may not exceed the stated Contract price except by approved Change Order.

Draws. Lender does not advance funds for deposits, up-front materials, or stored materials. Draws are funded on work-in-place only. Maximum 6 post-closing inspections/draws + 1 final. Final disbursement no less than 10% of contract amount.

Change Orders. Any change to price, work, or contract time must be in a Change Order signed by Borrower and Builder, and approved by Lender in writing before work begins.

Insurance. Builder maintains General Liability and Workers' Compensation throughout construction. Builder's Risk maintained throughout construction.
